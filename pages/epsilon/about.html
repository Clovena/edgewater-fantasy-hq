About Epsilon

League rules, format, and platform details.

Epsilon Fantasy Football was established in 2020 as an offshoot of a larger fraternity league, falling under new leadership while retaining many members from the original fraternity league. Epsilon takes its name from Sigma Phi Epsilon, honoring its fraternity origins, while also nodding to the mathematical symbol Îµ, representing a positive but indeterminate amount, in recognition of dear friends who were not brothers but have become equally valued members of the community.

Epsilon serves as a casual redraft league where long-time friends compete annually, with rosters reset and drafted from scratch each season. The league is free-to-play but awards the victors a traveling trophy, serving as a tangible artifact that passes from champion to champion. More recently, members have begun gathering once a year for an in-person retreat and offline draft, adding to the traditions of the league. This combination of history, friendship, and accessible competition has made Epsilon a cherished fantasy league.
